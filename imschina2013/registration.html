    This registration site is for non-local participants only (local participants are defined as those who reside in mainland China). All local participants can register this conference onsite only.


    For IMS-members, the registration fee is $300, a reduced fee of $200 for full time students, and $150 for every accompanying person.


    For non-IMS members, the registration fee is $350. If you wish to join the IMS, follow the link for conference registration below or visit www.imstat.org.


    The registration includes lecture, banquet, and the first day shuttle transportation between hotel and conference site. The registration is refundable before 12:00 noon June 1, 2013 (EST, USA), but not after that.


    The deadline for online registration is May 7, 2013. Onsite registration will be available on the conference site between June 30 and July 4, 2013, but there is a $50 extra charge.


    Due to a limit on room capacity, we have to have a stringent cap of 14 contributed talks by nonlocal speakers. If you are interested in giving a contributed talk, please note that we have a first come first serve policy, measured by the time of completing the abstract submission.


    If you are ready, click here to register.
